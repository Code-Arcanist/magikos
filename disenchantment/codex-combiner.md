# Codex Combiner

Using the power of the arcane enchantments, the Codex Combiner takes a stack of [Codex of Enchantment](codex-of-enchantment) and imbues a new codex with even more powerful enchantments.

Current recipe is at a 2:1 ratio, so two Codexes are taken out of the input stack and appear as a single upgraded Codex in the output.
